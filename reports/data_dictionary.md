- Data Dictionary

- `amfi_code` stores the unique mutual fund scheme identifier.

- `date` represents the NAV recorded date.

- `nav` contains the Net Asset Value of the scheme.

- `investor_id` stores the unique investor identifier.

- `transaction_date` represents the date of transaction.

- `transaction_type` defines the type of investment transaction.

- `amount_inr` stores the transaction amount in INR.

- `state`, `city`, and `city_tier` store investor location details.

- `age_group` and `gender` store demographic information.

- `annual_income_lakh` represents investor annual income in lakhs.

- `payment_mode` stores the transaction payment method.

- `kyc_status` indicates investor KYC verification status.

- `scheme_name` stores the mutual fund scheme name.

- `fund_house` represents the asset management company.

- `category` defines the mutual fund category.

- `plan` stores the scheme plan type.

- `return_1yr_pct`, `return_3yr_pct`, and `return_5yr_pct` represent scheme returns across different durations.

- `benchmark_3yr_pct` stores benchmark return values.

- `alpha`, `beta`, `sharpe_ratio`, and `sortino_ratio` represent fund performance and risk metrics.

- `std_dev_ann_pct` stores annualized volatility.

- `max_drawdown_pct` represents the maximum decline in scheme value.

- `aum_crore` stores Assets Under Management in crores.

- `expense_ratio_pct` represents the scheme expense ratio.

- `morningstar_rating` stores scheme rating values.

- `risk_grade` defines the investment risk category.
